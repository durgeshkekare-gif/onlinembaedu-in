Verdict on DSU Online MBA

DSU online MBA is a valid degree, but it's hard to recommend over alternatives at a similar or marginally higher price point. Jain University Online (NAAC A++, Bangalore-based like DSU, ~₹1L) offers a better NAAC grade at a similar fee. Chandigarh University (~₹90K, NAAC A+) is comparable in price with stronger accreditation. If Bangalore brand is important to you, Jain University or a slightly higher budget for Manipal MUJ will serve you better long-term.
