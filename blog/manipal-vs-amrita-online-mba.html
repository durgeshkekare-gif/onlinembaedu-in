Manipal vs Amrita Online MBA 2026 — Head-to-Head Comparison

Manipal MUJ vs Amrita online MBA — NIRF ranking, WES recognition, specialisations, fees, placement and who should choose which in 2026.

Manipal MUJ and Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham are India's two highest-ranked online MBA providers — both NIRF top-10, NAAC A++ and WES recognised. Here is a complete comparison to help you choose.

NIRF Ranking

Manipal MUJ: NIRF #4 University (Overall). Amrita: NIRF #7 University (Overall). Both are top-10 nationally — a negligible difference for most employers.

Specialisations

Manipal MUJ offers 13 specialisations including Finance, Marketing, HR, Retail, Healthcare, Analytics, Logistics, Banking & Insurance. Amrita offers 5 specialisations: Finance, Marketing, HR, AI & Business Analytics, Fintech. If you need a niche specialisation, Manipal wins. If you want Fintech or AI & Analytics, Amrita is unique.

WES Recognition

Both are WES recognised. Equal for USA/Canada career purposes.

Study Abroad

Amrita uniquely offers an optional study abroad module in the USA or Europe. Manipal does not have this. For internationally ambitious professionals, this is a differentiator.

Fee

Manipal: ₹1.8L. Amrita: ₹1.76L. Effectively the same.

Verdict

Choose Manipal if you want more specialisation options and the most established brand. Choose Amrita if you want AI/Fintech specialisation or the study abroad option, and/or if you are targeting jobs in the USA or Canada.
