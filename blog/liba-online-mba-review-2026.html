LIBA Online MBA Review 2026 — Honest Review

LIBA — what it is and where it stands

LIBA (Loyola Institute of Business Administration) is part of Loyola College, Chennai — one of India's oldest and most respected autonomous colleges. The LIBA brand is strong in Tamil Nadu, particularly in Chennai's corporate sector. However, for online MBA specifically, LIBA is an AICTE-approved institution rather than a UGC-DEB approved online MBA guide university — an important distinction for degree validity.

When to choose LIBA, when not to

Choose LIBA if: You're specifically targeting Chennai/Tamil Nadu organisations where LIBA alumni network is active, you want the Loyola-LIBA brand association for your specific industry, and the UGC degree vs AICTE distinction doesn't matter for your use case (private sector roles, not government).

Choose Manipal MUJ or Amrita instead if: You need a fully valid UGC degree (government jobs, Canada PR via WES, PhD admissions), you want better value (₹1.35L vs ₹2–3L), or your career goals are pan-India or global rather than Tamil Nadu-specific.
