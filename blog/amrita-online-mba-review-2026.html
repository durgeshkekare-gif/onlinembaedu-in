Amrita University Online MBA Review 2026 — Fee, NAAC A++ & Is It Worth It?

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham online MBA — overview

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is one of India's most respected private universities — NAAC A++ (highest grade), NIRF #7 University ranking and strong research reputation. The online MBA from Amrita is delivered under UGC-DEB approval, making it a valid degree for employment in India. Fee approximately ₹2–3L over 2 years. The programme is delivered through the Amrita Online platform with live faculty sessions, industry projects and placement support.

What makes Amrita distinctive is its strong research culture — the university has among the highest publication rates among private Indian universities. The MBA faculty bring both academic rigour and industry experience. For professionals wanting a credible NAAC A++ online MBA with a strong institutional reputation, Amrita is consistently among the top choices in 2026.

Fee structure and payment options

The Amrita online MBA total fee is approximately ₹2–3L depending on specialisation and batch. The programme runs over 4 semesters (2 years) with per-semester fee payment. EMI options are available through enrollment partners at 0% interest for qualified applicants. Application fee is separate (approximately ₹1,000–2,000). Exam fee for proctored online assessments is charged per semester.

Specialisations available

Amrita online MBA offers specialisations in: Marketing Management, Financial Management, Human Resource Management, Operations Management, Business Analytics, Data Science & AI, Healthcare Management, and Supply Chain Management. The Business Analytics and Healthcare Management specialisations are particularly strong given Amrita's research strengths in these areas.

Is Amrita online MBA worth it?

For professionals who value institutional reputation and NAAC grade, Amrita's A++ rating and NIRF #7 University ranking make it one of the most credible online MBA options available. The main consideration is fee — at ₹2–3L, it costs more than Manipal MUJ (₹1.35L). If WES recognition is critical (for Canadian immigration), verify current WES status before enrolling as recognition policies update periodically. For professionals in healthcare, biotech or research-adjacent sectors, Amrita's institutional reputation carries additional weight. Also consider the salary impact of MBA credentials when evaluating your investment.
