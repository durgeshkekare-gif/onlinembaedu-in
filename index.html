Why online MBA

Why an online MBA makes sense in 2026

UGC-DEB has made online degrees equivalent to campus degrees — here's what that means for you.

🎓
Same degree, different delivery
UGC-DEB certified online MBA degrees are equivalent to campus degrees for all private sector employment. Same transcript, same credential.
💰
80% cheaper than campus MBA
Campus MBA from a comparable institution costs ₹8–15L. Online MBA from the same university tier costs ₹1.1–2.5L. Same outcome, fraction of the cost.
📅
No career break needed
Weekend and evening live sessions. Keep your job, keep your salary, keep your momentum — and add a UGC degree in 24 months.
🌍
WES recognised for abroad
Manipal and Amrita online MBA degrees are WES (World Education Services) recognised — valid for jobs and higher education in USA and Canada.
📈
Real salary impact
Alumni surveys show 30–50% salary growth within 2 years of completing an online MBA from a top UGC-DEB university.
🏆
Top NAAC accreditation
Manipal, Amrita, Jain and Symbiosis all hold NAAC A++ or A+++ — the highest quality assurance grade in Indian higher education.
