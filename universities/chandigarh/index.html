Chandigarh University is NIRF ranked #26 nationally and holds NAAC A+ accreditation. At ₹1.1L total fee, it is the most affordable online MBA from a nationally NIRF-ranked university. Frequently asked questions

Yes. Chandigarh University's online MBA is UGC-DEB approved, making it equivalent to a campus MBA degree for private sector employment in India. All HR departments of major Indian companies accept UGC-DEB degrees.
Any graduate from a UGC-recognised university is eligible. No entrance exam (CAT, MAT, GMAT) is required. Fresh graduates and working professionals are both eligible. Minimum 50% in graduation is preferred by some universities but not strictly enforced.
Classes are live online — typically weekend sessions (Saturday and Sunday) with some weekday evening lectures. Sessions are also recorded and available for replay. All study material is digital. You can attend from anywhere with a stable internet connection.
Total fee is ₹1.1L. EMI: ₹4,600/month. Education loan options are also available from banks (HDFC Credila, Avanse, SBI) with Section 80E tax benefit on interest paid.
