About Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is an Institution of Eminence — one of only 20 Indian universities to hold this designation from the Ministry of Education. Ranked NIRF #7 overall, NAAC A++ accredited and WES recognised. Their online MBA includes an optional study abroad module in the USA or Europe — unique among affordable Indian online MBA programs.

Frequently asked questions

Yes. Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham's online MBA is UGC-DEB approved, making it equivalent to a campus MBA degree for private sector employment in India. All HR departments of major Indian companies accept UGC-DEB degrees.
Any graduate from a UGC-recognised university is eligible. No entrance exam (CAT, MAT, GMAT) is required. Fresh graduates and working professionals are both eligible. Minimum 50% in graduation is preferred by some universities but not strictly enforced.
Classes are live online — typically weekend sessions (Saturday and Sunday) with some weekday evening lectures. Sessions are also recorded and available for replay. All study material is digital. You can attend from anywhere with a stable internet connection.
