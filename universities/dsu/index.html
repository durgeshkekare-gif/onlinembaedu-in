About Dayananda Sagar University (DSU)

DSU offers India's most accessible blended Executive MBA degree — a full UGC-recognised MBA degree (not just a certificate) with alternate weekend on-campus sessions in Bengaluru combined with flexible online classes. 11 specialisations. Specifically designed for working professionals who want a formal Executive MBA degree at an accessible cost of ₹2.5L.

Frequently asked questions

Yes. Dayananda Sagar University (DSU)'s online MBA is UGC-DEB approved, making it equivalent to a campus MBA degree for private sector employment in India. All HR departments of major Indian companies accept UGC-DEB degrees.
Any graduate from a UGC-recognised university is eligible. No entrance exam (CAT, MAT, GMAT) is required. Fresh graduates and working professionals are both eligible. Minimum 50% in graduation is preferred by some universities but not strictly enforced.
Classes are live online — typically weekend sessions (Saturday and Sunday) with some weekday evening lectures. Sessions are also recorded and available for replay. All study material is digital. You can attend from anywhere with a stable internet connection.
Total fee is ₹2.5L. EMI: 5 instalments. Education loan options are also available from banks (HDFC Credila, Avanse, SBI) with Section 80E tax benefit on interest paid.
