About Bharati Vidyapeeth University (BVDU)

Bharati Vidyapeeth Deemed University (BVDU) is one of Maharashtra's most established institutions — founded in 1964 with campuses across Pune, Mumbai, Nashik, Kolhapur and beyond. UGC-DEB approved and AICTE recognised. Their online MBA 2.0 includes a summer internship for real-world exposure and dual specialisation option. Strong Maharashtra recognition.

Frequently asked questions

Yes. Bharati Vidyapeeth University (BVDU)'s online MBA is UGC-DEB approved, making it equivalent to a campus MBA degree for private sector employment in India. All HR departments of major Indian companies accept UGC-DEB degrees.
Any graduate from a UGC-recognised university is eligible. No entrance exam (CAT, MAT, GMAT) is required. Fresh graduates and working professionals are both eligible. Minimum 50% in graduation is preferred by some universities but not strictly enforced.
